Broadband Calculation Tool: Countries is designed for technology selection and calculation costs to connect unconnected localities to the broadband transport backbone and cost of building broadband access networks in the such localities.

Calculation results will be presented in the form of “Country Blueprints” with the summary of analysis and calculations.

The Tool is based on the special localities Database that prepared on the basis of different open geodata projects (OpenStreetMap, GeoNames etc.) and ITU Broadband Maps.
